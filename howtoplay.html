  1. Scan the PLAY GAME Card to visit EchoTales web app.
  2. Shuffle the 9 Fragment Cards and place them face down.
  3. Flip over the 3 Chapter Cards and palcce them in a row.
  4. Click on each item to listen to its story.
  5. Work together (or solo!) to match each item to the correct chapter in the story.
